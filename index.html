Biography

My name is Tao Huang (黄涛). I am a tenure-track assistant professor in John Hopcropt Center for Computer Science, School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University since 2025. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at The University of Sydney, advised by Prof. Chang Xu. Before that, I obtained the Bachelor's degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and then spent two years as a Researcher at SenseTime.

My major research interests lie within efficient deep learning algorithms and their applications in computer vision tasks:

  • Efficient architectures: NAS, handcrafts ...
  • Efficient training: training acceleration, KD, PEFT ...
  • Efficient data: self-supervised learning, generated training contents ...
  • Efficient inference: pruning, dynamic networks ...
  • Efficient large models: VLMs, Generative Models, VLAs ...
