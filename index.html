Biography

My name is Tao Huang (é»„æ¶›). I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at The University of Sydney, advised by Prof. Chang Xu. Before that, I was a Researcher at SenseTime, supervised by Dr. Shan You. In 2020, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China.


My major research interests lie within efficient machine learning algorithms and their applications in computer vision tasks:

  • Efficient architectures: NAS, handcrafts, pruning, Rep ...
  • Efficient training: training acceleration, KD, PEFT ...
  • Efficient data: SSL, zero/low-shot learning, generated training contents ...
  • Efficient inference: cache optimization, quantization, dynamic networks ...
  • Efficient large models: LLMs, VLMs, Generative Models ...
